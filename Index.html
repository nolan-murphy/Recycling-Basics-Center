Recycling Tips

  • Tip 1: Only recycle clean recyclables (clean your recyclables before placing them in the recycling bin)
  • Tip 2: Recycle goods with the universal recycling symbol
  • Tip 3: Reduce your use of single-use plastics
  • Tip 4: Reuse items when possible
  • Tip 5: Buy products made from recycled materials
